ABOUT ROAD TO COP 26

Indonesia submits New 2030 Climate Targets and First Long-Term Climate Strategy ahead of COP26. The commitment includes positive new measures on adaptation and resilience and specific targets on restoration of peatlands and degraded land, as what’s needed to meet the goals of The Paris Agreement.
To support the nation’s climate action commitment towards COP26 in Glasgow early November 2021, Katadata and Landscape Indonesia will conduct a campaign and online event:
